About us




Our vision is to create an easy, convenient, and “uber-like” food delivery app that allows the community to search of various restaurants and choose their food to be delivered.


Our mission is to assist the food business owner to expose and advertise their establishments and to promote their newly created products. To ease the problems of the busy community by delivering their food right at their doorstep.
